About Linus Tattoos Studio

Linus Tattoo Studio is where art meets skin. Our team of skilled artists specializes in custom designs, from bold traditional pieces to intricate minimalist tattoos.

We’re more than a studio—we’re a community of creators dedicated to making your vision a reality. With a focus on quality, hygiene, and creativity, we’ve built a reputation as the go-to spot for ink enthusiasts.
